Ingredients:
 - 1 pound ground beef
 - 1 slice stale bread
 - 1 small onion, finely-grated
 - 2 tablespoons breadcrumbs
 - 2 tablespoons oil
 - 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1 teaspoon pepper
 - 1 teaspoon oregano
 - 1 cup flour
 - 1 cup olive oil


Directions:
 1. Soak bread in water and squeeze hard to drain.
 2. Add ground meat, bread crumbs, oil or melted butter, onion, parsley, salt and pepper.
 3. Mix thoroughly, and form into 1-inch thick patties, sprinkle with salt, pepper, and oregano.
 4. Roll in flour and fry in hot oil.
 5. Serve hot with a salad or a yogurt sauce if preferred.
